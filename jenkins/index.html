Jenkins for Continuous Integration with fastlane

Why integrate fastlane and Jenkins?

  • automate more tasks with less work
  • leverage the best of Jenkins with all of the plugins available for fastlane
  • reduce overhead and give your development team time to focus on code, not build tasks
  • learn early when a updates are causing build and delivery problems

Please refer to documentation on Jenkins for further details on setting up continuous integration with fastlane.
